Frequently Asked Questions

What is addiction?

Addiction is a condition where a person becomes dependent on a substance or behavior, leading to an inability to control its use despite negative consequences.

How long does addiction recovery take?

The duration of addiction recovery varies, but it often takes several months to years, with ongoing support needed for long-term success.

What is a support group, and how can it help in recovery?

Our support groups work on 24*7 basis and provide all the necessary support to recovery from your addiction.

Are there medications available to help with addiction treatment?

Yes, medications can assist in addiction treatment by reducing cravings and withdrawal symptoms.

How can I find addiction treatment centers or professionals in my area?

You can find the nearest treatment center in recovery section.

What is the role of therapy and counseling in addiction recovery?

Therapy and counseling play a crucial role in addiction recovery by addressing underlying issues, providing coping strategies, and fostering emotional support.

Are there specific programs or resources for adolescents or teenagers dealing with addiction?

Yes, there are specialized programs and resources designed to address the unique needs of adolescents and teenagers dealing with addiction, often tailored to their age group and developmental stages.

Are there alternative therapies or holistic approaches to addiction recovery?

We support both approach based on your choice.

What role do nutrition and exercise play in addiction recovery?

Nutrition and exercise support addiction recovery by promoting physical and mental well-being, reducing cravings, and improving overall health.

What are some healthy coping strategies for dealing with stress and triggers during addiction recovery?

Healthy coping strategies include meditation, exercise, therapy, and a strong support network to manage stress in recovery.
